Achieving my dream of becoming a graphic designer requires commitment, continuous learning and a clear plan. Below are the essential steps I am taking to turn this dream into reality:

  1. Complete my Bachelor's Degree in New Media Communication
    Studying at Universiti Malaysia Perlis (UniMAP) provides me with the academic foundation and practical skills in communication and design.
  2. Develop strong design skills through continuous practice
    I regularly work on personal and academic projects to improve my creativity, use of software and overall design techniques.
  3. Master industry-standard design tools
    Proficiency in tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign is essential to stay competitive in the design industry.
  4. Build an online portfolio
    Showcasing my work through a professional website or platform helps me demonstrate my skills to future employers or clients.
  5. Complete internships or freelance projects
    Gaining real-world experience through internships or freelance jobs allows me to learn how the industry works while building my resume.
  6. Attend workshops, webinars and design events
    These opportunities help me stay updated with trends and connect with experienced designers for guidance and networking.
  7. Apply for jobs in creative industries
    After graduation, I aim to start working in media agencies, marketing companies or design studios to begin my professional journey.
